# Support SLA Demo

## Priority Levels

P1 incidents affect a production-critical workflow for many users. The first response target for a P1 incident is 15 minutes during staffed hours.

## Escalation

Support should escalate P1 and P2 incidents to the owning engineering team with customer impact, reproduction steps, logs, and recent deployment context.

## Customer Updates

For active P1 incidents, customer-facing updates should be sent every 30 minutes until mitigation is complete.
