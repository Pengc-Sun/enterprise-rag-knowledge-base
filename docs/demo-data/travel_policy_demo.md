# Travel Policy Demo

## International Travel Requests

International travel requests should be submitted at least 14 days before departure. The request should include destination, business purpose, cost center, estimated airfare, hotel estimate, and manager approval.

## Meal Allowance

The maximum meal allowance for approved business travel is GBP 40 per day. Alcohol is excluded unless explicitly approved by the finance team for a client event.

## Hotel Reimbursement

Hotel reimbursement requires an itemized hotel receipt and proof of payment. Room service may be reimbursed only when it fits within the daily meal allowance.

## Missing Receipt Exception

If a taxi fare receipt is missing, the employee must submit a missing receipt declaration and manager approval. Finance may reject repeated exceptions.
