# Basic Test Policy

## Travel Meals

For approved business travel, the daily meal allowance is GBP 40 per day. Employees should submit meal claims with the travel date, city, and business purpose.

## Hotel Reimbursement

Hotel reimbursement requires an itemized hotel receipt and proof of payment. The receipt must show the guest name, stay dates, room charge, taxes, and total amount paid.

## Lost Laptop

If an employee loses a company laptop, they must report it to Security Operations within 30 minutes. The report should include the last known location, device owner, and whether confidential data may be stored on the device.

## Support Priority

A P1 support incident affects a production-critical workflow for many users. The first response target for a P1 incident is 15 minutes during staffed hours.

## Engineering Review

Production code changes require at least one peer review and passing CI checks before merge. Security-sensitive changes require an additional approval from the owning team.
